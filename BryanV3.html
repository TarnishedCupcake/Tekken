1. Introduction & Archetype Profile

Guide Name

Comprehensive Bryan Guide: Version 3

Character Archetype

  • Type: Counter-hit Pressure / Technical Execution
  • Playstyle Range: Small Tekken Control → CH Baiting → Taunt Execution Flow
  • Weaknesses: High execution demand, weak panic tools, requires strong timing

Archetype Breakdown

Bryan Fury is a pressure-based technical character who thrives on timing, spacing, and reward from counter-hits. His neutral pokes are safe and deliberate, but his payoff comes from opponent mistakes, whiff traps, and tight frame traps.

He can be played through:

  • Small Tekken: Low-risk 1s, D/F+1s, and D/B+3s to create tempo breaks
  • Whiff Baiter: Backdash into B+1 or D/F+2 with reward from opponents overextending
  • Heat Specialist: Convert one Heat starter into fatigue loops, cancel traps, or full wallsplat pressure
  • Taunt Master: Round-ending presence at the wall with frame-perfect setups and mental lockouts

Tag Glossary (Used Throughout the Guide)

  • [Poke] – Low-risk hit confirm, usually safe on block
  • [Approach] – Moves that close space and force blocks
  • [CH Tool] – Counter-hit setup, trap, or launcher
  • [Combo Starter] – Begins a route on normal or CH
  • [Wall Carry] – Extends combo to corner or wall
  • [Heat Engager] – Activates Heat on hit
  • [Cancel Routing] – Can be canceled into Taunt or QCB stance
  • [Movement Tool] – Improves Bryan's axis shift or evasion
  • [Install] – Refers to Snake Eyes state

Execution Profile

  • Execution Difficulty: 9.5/10
  • Combos: Very technical; wall and taunt combos require strict timing
  • Pressure Game: Requires rhythm, cancel management, and fakeouts
  • Heat Management: Precise routing, or you will lose momentum instantly
