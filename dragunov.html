Comprehensive Dragunov Guide

Part 1: Introduction

Archetype Overview

Dragunov is a Pressure Grappler / Control Brawler who thrives at close range. He excels in forcing turn-based interactions through relentless frame traps, strong command throws, and tight mid-check pressure. While his approach tools are linear, once in, he can condition opponents into high-risk guessing games using SNK transitions and Heat-enhanced pressure.

Execution Tier

Moderate Difficulty. Dragunov requires clean buffering (especially for WR/F+2,1), SNK cancel usage, fast reaction to counter-hits, and advanced movement to maintain pressure while denying opponent retaliation. His ceiling scales dramatically with Heat optimization and matchup-specific timing traps.

Strengths

  • + Frames on key strings (e.g., F+2,1, 1,2~SNK)
  • Outstanding CH reward from mids (e.g., CH B+1)
  • Wall carry with reliable enders into spike oki
  • Strong throw mix with Oki pressure on break
  • SNK stance creates a layered offense system
  • Heat routing adds pressure resets and damage

Weaknesses

  • Linear approach tools ‚Äî weak tracking mids
  • Can be sidestepped Right post-SNK or F+2,1
  • Has no real 50/50 launcher threat
  • Struggles against life-lead turtles
  • Relies on frame traps, not mix, to open up

Offensive Game Plan

Dragunov thrives on pressure cycles ‚Äî abusing frame advantage to set up stance transitions or reset with throws. Heat Dash after F+2,1 or 1,2~SNK lets him force mix layers. Use delay timing on SNK 2 to catch fuzzy defense, or threaten tick throws to collapse opponent guard. At the wall, SNK 1 ‚Üí throw becomes a deadly loop.

Defensive Game Plan

On defense, Dragunov favors spacing traps and counter-hit punishes. B+1 and D/F+2 are key for interrupting pressure. Use backdash into SNK cancel for baiting jabs or mids, and WS+4 to safely interrupt risky lows. In Heat, leverage armor trades to reverse momentum when behind.

Part 4: Stances & Pressure

SNK Stance System Overview

  • SNK (Sneak): A crouch-based stance entered from select moves (1,2~SNK, 2,1~SNK, F+2,1~SNK).
  • SNK attacks include SNK 1 (mid), SNK 2 (low spike), and SNK 3 (low sweep).
  • Can cancel into SNK from strings to maintain pressure or create mixups.
  • SNK stance transitions maintain Dragunov‚Äôs ability to apply + frames into layered offense.

SNK Entry Transitions

  • 1,2~SNK ‚Äì Dragunov‚Äôs fastest transition; safe, plus on hit.
  • 2,1~SNK ‚Äì Mixup-enabled on hit, risky on block.
  • F+2,1~SNK ‚Äì Converts into Heat Dash or SNK for massive pressure reset.

Stance Cancel Logic

Stance transitions are cancelable into movement or attack depending on recovery window:

  • 1,2~SNK ‚Üí can cancel into backdash or SNK 1 mid.
  • SNK 1~Cancel ‚Üí repeat SNK entry for pressure loop (fatigue builds over time).
  • F+2,1~SNK ‚Üí SNK 2 low ‚Üí block ‚Üí + oki if delayed.

  • Overuse of SNK 1 ‚Üí SNK 2 ‚Üí cancel leads to lower reaction threshold from opponents.
  • To reset pressure: SNK 1 ‚Üí throw, or break rhythm with WR+2 ‚Üí jab trap.
  • Watch for players who step SNK ‚Üí use D+2/WR+2 to reset guard.
