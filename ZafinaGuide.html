Comprehensive Zafina Guide

Patch: Tekken 8 Season 2.03
Archetype: Evasive Stance Pressure | Reactive Trapper
Execution: Intermediate–High

1. Introduction & Archetype Profile

Zafina is a stance-centric character who excels at evasive movement, long-limbed pressure, and unpredictable rhythm resets. With access to three key stances — Scarecrow (SCR), Tarantula (TRT), and Mantis (MNT) — she traps the opponent into panic decisions using unique hitboxes and layered flow options.

Strengths

  • Top-tier evasion using stance transitions
  • Unconventional timing and visual mixups
  • Long-range pokes and solid tracking mids
  • Great wall carry and trap layering at the corner
  • Strong Heat synergy via stance cancels and chip setups

Weaknesses

  • Linear without stance pressure active
  • Weak standing block punishment vs some lows
  • Limited reward from safe pokes
  • Vulnerable to quick mash retaliation if stance timing is misused

Playstyle Types

  • Rushdown Flow Layer: Overwhelms with +frames into stance traps (SCR df+1, SCR 3+4)
  • Defensive Anchor: Uses U+2 and whiff punishing to stop pressure, then reclaims space with stance cancels
  • Mix-Up Depth Swap: Abuses stance re-entry and feint loops in Heat for guaranteed traps or evasions
  • Neutral Extension: Mid-range control with df+1, df+4, f+3~SCR, and ss+1 to maintain mental stack
